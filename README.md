# J&D Mobile Detailing — Business Website

A fully functional business website built for **J&D Mobile Detailing**, a mobile car 
detailing company based in Birmingham, Alabama. The site serves as the central hub for 
the business — professionally showcasing services, building trust with customers, and 
removing friction from the booking process.

🌐 **Live Site:** [jdmobiledetailing.com](https://jdmobiledetailing.com)

---

## The Goal

The client needed more than just a website — they needed a single place that matched 
the quality of their services and made it effortless for customers to book. Previously, 
the booking process involved separate links and platforms, creating friction that 
risked losing potential customers.

The solution: bring everything together in one place.

---

## Process

1. **Discovery** — Booking calls with the business owner to understand their goals, 
target audience, and brand identity
2. **Planning** — Designing and outlining a project sketch aligned with the business vision
3. **Development** — Building the full website with a focus on performance, 
responsiveness, and user experience
4. **Integration** — Connecting third-party booking software directly into the site, 
eliminating the need for external links

---

## Features

- **Services showcase** — professionally displays all detailing packages and pricing
- **Before & After gallery** — visual proof of work quality to build customer trust
- **Integrated booking system** — customers can book directly on the site without 
being redirected elsewhere
- **SEO optimized** — structured for search engine visibility to help the business 
get found locally
- **Performance optimized** — fast load times across all devices
- **Fully responsive** — seamless experience on mobile, tablet, and desktop

---

## Tech Stack

- React
- Tailwind CSS
- Third-party booking API integration

---

## Outcome

The business owner is happy with the final result. The website now reflects the 
professionalism and quality of J&D Mobile Detailing's services, consolidates the 
customer journey into a single platform, and removes the booking friction that 
previously cost the business potential customers.
